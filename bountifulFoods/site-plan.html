Site Purpose

The purpose for this website is to encourage healthy eating. We want to create opportunities to access healthy foods and recipes quicker and easier. We will have a page where each user can create their own smoothies. We are a small business that specializes in organic locally grown foods. The mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas of California.
